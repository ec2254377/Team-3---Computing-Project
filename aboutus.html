ABOUT US

We believe conservation is best advanced when diverse teams, partners and local communities are working together to better understand and protect our planet.



Sustainable Growth Partners is a global environmental non-profit working to create a world where nature and people can thrive.

Founded in Greece in 2007, Sustainable Growth Partners(SGP) has grown to become one of the most effective and innovative environmental organisations in the world. Thanks to more than a million members and dedicated leaders, the company has been able to focus on creating green spaces, promoting sustainable clothing and conserving natural resources.


OUR MISSION

Crafting a Cleaner, Greener Tomorrow.

To conserve natural resources and promote a sustainable future.We aim to overcome the biggest challenge facing humanity and reverse global warming.

OUR VISION

Building a Better World, One Business at a Time.

A world where the diversity of life thrives and people act to conserve nature for its own sake and its ability to fulfil our needs and enrich our lives.


OUR History



Sustainable Growth Partners has been operating since 2007 with over 100 leading scientists, committed citizens and dedicated leaders with a vision of protecting and caring for nature. The company has been able to change the lives of over 3000 people across 40 different countries and has since seen a rise in volunteers within the company. Since the development of SGP there has been a 4% decrease in climate change issues from actions that have been made by the company.

Sustainable Growth Partners Throughout the years

Before the development of SGP in 2007, there had been adverse effects on the environment with a 5% incease in waste pollution and global warming in gGreece. After Mike, Sebastian and Jollene noticed the impacts of greenhouse gases across the planet, they made the decision to start their company with only 5 members in it, with hopes of introducing policies and actions against global warming.

2007 , Foundation of Sustainable Growth Partners

The creation of the company created shock waves in the sustainable development industry as they began to change the way that the world views natural resources and materials. About 50% of the population including the company, began to diversify their approach towards sorucing natural ingredients with a goal of restoring biodivesity.

2007 - 2012, Changing the World

The company has been able to tranform a degrading rainforest in Africa, saving over 2600 species from extinction. They have been have to feed over 50 000 children in the Middle East and provide clothing supplies towards the maternity ward and Hujlik Hospital in Tanzania. Since their impact, SGP has been able to appear has the top 3 company that has changed the world.

2012- Present, Sustainable Growth Partners Impact

    Meet Our Volunteers




    CREATING GREENER SPACES EVERYDAY

    Biodivesity and Nature Volunteers

    We’re responsible for conserving our natural resources while we still have time. We invest over 20 hours every week to visit animals that are at risk of extincition and provide knowledge on the adverse effects of using resources that are at risk of extinction. Our team has been able to save pandas throught protests every week.






    STRENGTHNENING FOOD SECURITY

    Food Security Volunteers

    We’re a small (but mighty!) team that is responsible for collecting food donations in from organisations and supermarkets with a goal of feeding those in need. We have been able to feed over 3000 people from donations we receive each week and have since reduced malnutrition rates by 50%.






    KEEP OUR LAND CLEAN AT ALL TIMES

    Recycling Volunteers

    We’re the recycling team, we are responsible for keeping our environment clean at all costs. We spent over 40 hours per week recycling materials such as plastic and boxes which are in turn used to produce some of our leading products.
