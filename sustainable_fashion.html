CREATING FABRICS THAT PROTECT THE EARTH

What Makes us Who We are

We love clothes. But there are some skeletons in the closet that can’t be hidden. The global fashion industry has a huge problem with pollution, waste and human rights abuses. Like you, we care for our planet, people and animals, and we want to live in a world that’s sustainable and fair. We are a group of campaigners, fashion professionals, scientists, writers and developers who have come together to drive that change.


ECO-FABRIC ACCOUNTABILITY

As a sustainable development company, we take pride in every source we take from the environment to sustain the Earth. We have managed to restore over 35% of rainforests in the Amazon by using the decomposed materials to develop our products.






MATERIAL SELECTION


Materials matter. They matter when it comes to the life of our clothes, or the way we feel as we live in them. But most importantly, they matter for our earth. That's why we source only the most comfortable and sustainable textiles on the market.


MAKING THE RIGHT CHOICE

We prioritize the use of eco-friendly materials such as organic Cotton, Hemp, Bamboo, Tercel and recycled Polyster for reducing environmental impact.



WHY COTTON ?

Cultivation of Organic Cotton is beneficital in terms of reduced pesticide use, water conservation, and soil health.
Additionally, Cotton is breathable, hypoallergenic and comfortable to wear, making it an ideal choice for eco-conscious consumers.



HEMP AS A FIBER

We also utilize Hemp for it's remarkable eco-friendly properties.
It requires minimal water and pesticides to grow, making it one of the most sustainable crops.
It is strong and durable resulting in long-lasting garments thus minimizing waste.

EXPLORE OUR ECO-PRODUCTION


Sustainable clothes are made in a way that's kind to the earth and those who call it home. That's why we work only with manufacturers and suppliers that are conscious of their environmental impact, and that guarantee a safe and respectful environment for their employees



ECO-DYEING

We believe in eco-friendly dyeing technique which involves the use of non-toxic, biodegradable, and renewable materials that have a minimal impact on the environment.
Natural dyes are derived from plant-based sources, such as roots, leaves, flowers, and fruits.


WASTE REDUCTION

We use minimal-waste design techniques to reduce waste throughout the production process.
This can involve using innovative pattern-making techniques to maximize fabric usage, as well as repurposing scraps into new products or accessories.


SUSTAINABLE FASHION

We consider the environmental impact at every stage of the production process of our eco-friendly clothing, from sourcing materials to manufacturing, distribution, and end-of-life disposal to reduce their environmental footprint and contribute to a more sustainable fashion industry.

RECYCLING ESSENTIALS



Quality Over Quantity

We refer to the principle of prioritizing well-made, durable garments over a larger quantity of cheaper, lower-quality items.

This concept emphasizes investing in pieces that are constructed with high-quality materials, superior craftsmanship, and timeless design, rather than buying a large number of disposable or trendy items.

The Right Test Of Time

We design our clothing with durability, longevity and recyclability in mind, encouraging customers to invest in quality pieces that withstand the test of time.

Creating timeless designs that won't go out of fashion quickly and can be easily repaired or recycled at the end of their life cycle.

Reduce Reuse Recycle

We come together with brands that are exploring circular economy models, where products are designed to be reused, repaired, or recycled at the end of their life cycle.

This helps to minimize waste and extend the lifespan of our clothing items.
